Welcome to Saint Clair Shores, MI!


The neighborhood I live in is in the city of Saint Clair Shores on lake Saint Clair. It is just outside of Detroit. There's a lot to do in my city, from boating and fishing to golfing at the local city golf course. There's a ton of local breweries and restaurants. Downtown Saint Clair Shores is full of local shops and restaurants. Come and visit some time!
